The column headings are:

Fun
Was this game fun to play. Would you want to come back and play it again?
Inno (innovation)
Did this game break new ground in terms of gameplay, genre or approach?
Prod (production)
Did the game have nice graphics? Did it include sound and music? Were these elements appropriate and balanced?
Avg (average score)
This is the score used to rank the entries and is the average of the above three scores.
Ents
The percentage of participants (users) that rated this entry.
DQ
The percentage of participants (users) who thought the entry broke the challenge rules.
